Real Life Minutely Fantasy Sports (RLMFS)

Experience the Thrill of Real-Time Fantasy Sports

Welcome to Real Life Minutely Fantasy Sports (RLMFS), the revolutionary way to engage with your favorite sports in real time. Unlike traditional fantasy sports, which operate on a daily or weekly basis, RLMFS brings you into the action every single minute of the game. Here's how it works and why it's a game-changer:

Real-Time Engagement

With RLMFS, the excitement never stops. As the Llamas and Unicorns battle it out in our tech-infused micro stadium, you can join the action by making real-time predictions. Watch the game live, analyze the players' performance, and set your own prop markets on the fly. Each minute brings a new opportunity to test your skills and instincts.

Peer-to-Peer Socialization

RLMFS isn't just about winning; it's about connecting. Our platform fosters a social gaming environment where you can challenge your friends and other fans in real-time. Make a prediction and see who among your peers can outplay the other by choosing higher or lower. This peer-to-peer interaction transforms the game into a communal experience, making each match more thrilling and engaging.

Short, Intense Games

We know your time is valuable, which is why our games are designed to be short and intense. Each game lasts only 10 minutes, ensuring that you can dive into the action without a long-term commitment. Whether you're on a break, at a bar, or just looking for a quick adrenaline rush, RLMFS fits perfectly into your schedule.

  • Easy to Play: Simple, straightforward gameplay that anyone can enjoy.

  • Accessible Anywhere: Play from your smartphone, at our tables, or anywhere you have internet access.
